## Changes made for SnapTrack

- Replaced Dime’s expense-tracking domain with a calorie and macro-tracking domain.
- Replaced the Core Data / iCloud source of truth with a Supabase-backed data layer.
- Reused and adapted Dime’s SwiftUI views, components, design system, widgets, and App Intents.
- Ported AI food-logging concepts and code patterns from Fud AI and the existing SnapTrack iOS app.
- Rebranded the app as **SnapTrack**.
